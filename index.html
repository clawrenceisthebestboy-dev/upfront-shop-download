Everything Your Shop Actually Needs

Not a bloated platform. Not a monthly subscription. Just the tools that run your shop — designed the way a shop actually works.

Instant Invoices & Estimates

Generate professional invoices and estimates in seconds. Print, email, or hand one to the customer. Convert estimates to invoices with one click.

Real-Time P&L Reports

See exactly where you stand — daily and monthly profit & loss broken down by parts and labor. Know your margin on every job, every day.

Parts Inventory Control

Track every part in stock. Set reorder points. Costs are automatically marked up using your tiered pricing table. Never lose a SKU again.

Technician Timeclock

Clock in and out from the software. Link time to specific jobs. See labor cost vs. labor revenue per job and per period.

Customer & Vehicle Records

Full customer history, vehicle details, VIN, plate, and service records all linked together. Find anything in seconds, not file drawers.

Service Reminders

Track upcoming inspection expirations and maintenance due dates. Keep customers coming back by knowing what's coming up before they do.

Runs on Your Computer

No cloud account. No monthly fee. No internet required at the shop. Your data stays on your machine, accessible any time — even when the Wi-Fi goes down.

Cryptographically Signed Updates

Every software update is signed with an Ed25519 key and verified before installation. You always know the update came from us and wasn't tampered with.

Built for the Way Shops Actually Work

Most shop software looks like it was designed by accountants who never changed their own oil. Up Front Shop was built by people who understand the bay, the parts desk, and the counter — because that's where the data comes from.

Tiered Part Pricing

Set your markup by cost bracket. Prices auto-fill on every invoice — accurate every time, no manual math.

Tax Calculated Automatically

Set your tax rate once. Every invoice applies it correctly to taxable parts and labor — no more手动 math errors.

Cash, Card, or Check — Done Your Way

Mark jobs paid in one step. Cash closes print the invoice and remove the job from the database — the way shops actually do cash business.

Automatic Invoice Numbering

E- numbers for estimates, I- numbers for invoices. Never double a number. Never wonder what comes next.

Delete Jobs with Audit Trail

Returns and corrections are tracked. When you restock parts, inventory is restored automatically.
